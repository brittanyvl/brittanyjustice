About

I started my career in sterile drug procurement and supply chain analytics, working directly with the realities of sourcing, billing, compliance, and shortages. That experience shaped how I think about data.

Today, I design intelligence and optimization systems that help healthcare organizations make defensible decisions in complex markets. I work end-to-end, from messy data ingestion to modeling to decision support.

I'm particularly interested in problems where pricing, regulation, and operational constraints interact in non-obvious ways.

If you're working on procurement strategy, supplier risk, or market intelligence in compound or sterile drugs, I'm always open to thoughtful conversations.
